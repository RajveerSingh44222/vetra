Engineered for Absolute Privacy

Security isn't a feature—it's our foundation. Discover the core values of Vetra.

Zero-Knowledge

Your master key never leaves your device. All encryption and decryption happen locally using web cryptography standards.

Military Grade Encryption

We use PBKDF2 for key derivation alongside AES-GCM 256-bit encryption. Your credentials remain unreadable to everyone, including us.

Cross-Platform Vault

Securely sync your encrypted credentials across all browsers and devices while retaining sole custody of the decryption keys.

Automatic Lock

Inactive tabs auto-lock instantly, wiping local keys from session memory. Your credentials remain safe even if your device is compromised.

Cryptographic Workflows

How we ensure complete zero-knowledge security on every device.

01

Key Stretching

Your Master Key is combined with a local salt and stretched using PBKDF2 with 600,000 iterations of SHA-256 to block brute-force attacks.

02

Local Encryption

Credentials are encrypted locally in browser memory via authenticated AES-GCM 256-bit cryptography before uploading anywhere.

03

Encrypted Backup

Only the resulting high-entropy ciphertext is synchronized. Vetra servers only hold encrypted blobs—never plain text passwords.
